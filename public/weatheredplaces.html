    Wupperthal Veldskoen Factory was founded in 1836. It is the oldest known opperating shoe factory in South Africa. Located in the remote Cedarberg, Wupperhal is a small remote village, only accessble by a gravel road that has been a Moravian mission station since 1865. The shoe fatory is one of only a few funtioning businesses with only a handful of men who work there. Also known as Vellies, Veldskoen are traditional Afrikaans shoes individually hand made from untanned leather and rawhide that are known for their durability able to handle the weathering of time.
