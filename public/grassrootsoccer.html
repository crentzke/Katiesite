• Grassroot Soccer is a Non Profit Organization that uses soccer based skills as a venue to educate children about HIV and AIDS. It provides African youth with the knowledge, life-skills and support to live HIV-free lives. Established in 2002, Grassroot have programs in Zimbabwe, South Africa and Zambia. This photographic documentary is a work in progress that tells the story about the work of the Grassroot Soccer Skills program in Khayelitsha, a township in the Cape Town area of South Africa. South Africa has one of the highest HIV rates in the world with over 5.7 million people out of it 49 million population living with the epidemic in 2009, according United Nations General Assembly Special Session Progress Report on HIV/AIDS in South Africa . In Khayalitsha alone, at least 70% of its 1.5 million inhabitants are infected with HIV, one of the highest HIV/AIDS infection rate in the country.
